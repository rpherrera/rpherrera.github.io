About

Welcome to my little corner of Internet. I am glad you came here and would be pleased if you just found something really useful. If so, please let me know.

Here is the place I choose to share some of my last experiences and research, which are mainly related to Operating Systems, Computer Networks, Systems Administration and Software Development.

Currently, I live and work in São Paulo, notably the most developed and influential city of Brazil in terms of I.T. landscape. It is been part of my daily basis acting as a DevOp Analyst and also, more recently, exerting the role of Technical Leadership on Infrastructure Team. The company which employs me is a Research and Development Startup known as Boo Labs, which develops E-Commerce products based on Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision and Machine Learning technologies.

In academic field of interest, I earned both Masters (MScCS) and Bachelors (BSCS) degrees in Computer Science, when studied in Computer Science Department from State University of Londrina. It is located in Paraná State, where I lived from the beginning of 2005 up to the middle of 2012 and within this time I developed some sort of research work on Computer Networks Security, Distributed Computing, In-Memory Data Grids, Relational Data Base Middlewares, Legacy Information Systems and Migration Models Applied to Cloud Computing.
